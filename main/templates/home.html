How it works

Just a few clicks for powerful views

There are many aspects that impact the needs and behavior of a place, and therefore how your business or organization will perform there. With just a few simple clicks, explore, compare and visualize location context with over 150 variables derived from the Census, the Economic Census and various other industry sources.

See how regions compare across numerous factors

Trying to get a view of how an economic factor differs across regions? Just select your region and relevant variables and go!

Get a snapshot of a particular place

How does your area stack up? See how it ranks compared to others on a host of dimensions

Find places that meet your criteria

Looking for places with a particular profile? Filter locations at the village level by numerous criteria and view them on a map or as downloadable lists.
