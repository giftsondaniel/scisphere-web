All places are not equal. Our algorithms can help you make better location choices for new branches and improve your business outcome as much as 200%. Just upload your location data and have almost immediate access to location selection scores and tools.

Upload addresses or lat/longs of your existing locations along with any associated performance metrics in our predefined template. Ex. Sales, Deposits, Productivity

Why does it need my data?

Companies and products cater to different customers and situations so we use your data to train our location selection model to your particular situation.

Note that our models currently work at the settlement level and not at the neighborhood level within cities. City neighborhood level analytics will be available in 2015.
Case StudyDownload

>100% potential improvement in branch performance with SciSphere's location selection intelligence, translating to over Rs. 120 Crores of additional
business per branch.

A fast growing private sector bank with aggressive expansion plans in small towns and rural areas was looking for an intelligent mechanism of branch placement that would ensure both speed to market and better branch performance. Currently the Bank has close to 200 branches of this type with a very wide spread of performance. The difference between the total business varies more than 4x across the branches. In addition, the Bank would prefer places where their business would have potential for a larger ratio of deposits to advances.

If you don't have lat/longs we can geocode each address for you to enable calculation of numerous associated geospatial parameters. Ex. 5km Bank branch density, distance to nearest highway

How long does this take?

For a few locations, it may take less than a day. For a lot of locations this could take up to 10 days.

Our automated algorithms that explore over 200 location variables are trained with your branch data to create a location selection model that best predicts your performance parameters. A different model can be created for each performance metric.

How do I know that your model works?

We train our model using part of your existing data and test it on the remaining part. By checking the model results against the actual performance of the test locations, we can tell you exactly how well it works.

Filter Locations by Model Score

We will provide you information on how well your model performs and integrate the model scores as one of your variables for all locations in your selected States so that you can visualize them in various ways on a map or in a list in the Explore tab.
